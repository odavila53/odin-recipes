Honey Chicken Recipe

This is a 12 minute chicken breast recipe with a 5 ingredient Honey Garlic Sauce!

This is a perfect recipe for a quick dinner idea for busy nights that's made with pantry staples


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Split chicken breast in half - so 2 breasts makes 4 pieces
  2. Dust chicken with flour (gives the chicken a crust)
  3. Sear the chicken on a hot oiled pan
  4. Add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, and honey
  5. Simmer until the sauce thickens. Then turn the chicken to coat in the amazing honey garlic sauce
  6. Enjoy!

Ground Turkey Recipe

A simple ground turkey recipe great for meal prepping! it only requires a few ingredients! Serve with rice and vegetables of your choosing for a quick and healthy meal!


Ingredients
  • Ground Turkey (The less fat the better)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ground Paprika
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oil

Steps
  1. Chop up half an onion and 3-4 cloves of garlic and it set aside
  2. Cut up the bell pepper into bite sized pieces
  3. Add oil to a hot pan and let it heat up
  4. Fry up the garlic and onion first and let it brown. Then add the bell pepper and mix everything together
  5. Add the ground beef to the pan and make it into little pieces. Mix everything together
  6. Add all the seasonings and let it cook until the ground beef is dark brown
  7. Enjoy!
